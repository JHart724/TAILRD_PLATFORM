TAILRD Heart is a HIPAA-compliant cardiovascular AI clinical decision support platform serving health systems. We use SES for transactional emails only: (1) password reset emails when users request reset, (2) account invitation emails when administrators invite new users to their hospital tenant, (3) security alert emails for unusual account activity, (4) MFA backup code delivery on enrollment. All emails are user-initiated or security-related. We do not send marketing or promotional content. Recipients are explicitly authorized users at customer health systems including Mount Sinai (NY), HCA Healthcare, Baylor Scott and White, and CommonSpirit Health. Volume estimate: 50-200 emails per day initially, scaling to 1000-5000 per day as we onboard health systems. We have full bounce and complaint handling: hard bounces immediately disable the user account; complaints trigger a security review. Our domain tailrd-heart.com is fully DKIM-signed (RSA 2048), has SPF, and DMARC quarantine policy.
